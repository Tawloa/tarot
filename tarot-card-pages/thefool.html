The Fool

Description

The fool is off on a new journey.

In this deck, the fool is looking back to the city he has just left, leaving the bright, artificial lights behind to travel into the dark. He is blinded at first, and the first steps are in shadow.

Aspects

  • New Beginnings
  • Leaving Known Structure
  • Chaos
  • The Creative Urge
  • The Roads are Open

Readings

  1. Lose the rules
  2. Let go of expectations
  3. Lose yourself to the journey
